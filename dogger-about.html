Dogger

 About rufs Pack life Legal

We are a group of creators who want to let the dogs out.

After spending cold, rainy evenings in the dog park waiting for an other dog to appear from the darkness, we got an idea. What if there was a way to let other dogs in the area know that the party was about to start?

Dogger is a mobile based community platform where you can send notifications to other dog owners about visiting the local dog park. You can find new friends for your dog and why not also for your self!
